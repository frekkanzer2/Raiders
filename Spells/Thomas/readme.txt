Grobus - Summons a Gobshell that inflicts damage at the start of its turn
	at each hero in its same line. The damage depends on the SE level
Evolution - Adds one SE on the targeted Gobshell + 100% heals it
Hypertension - The target Gobshell reaches max level of evolution, but
	it cannot transform itself. At the end of its turn, it will die
Scaphander - Kills a Gobshell to assign one SE to the other Gobshells
	on the field

Alerts
- SE = Steamer Energy that goes from 0 to 4 (4 = transformation)
- The Gobshell will transform in a Steamander, that will move and attack
